What I do

I am a Full Stack Web Development Student in my second week at Lambda School. Here is a general outline of my studies thus far!

HTML

HTML, Hypertext Markup Language is arguably the most important language of Web Development! It allows a programmer like myself to design the skeleton of a website for later design! I have had very small amounts of exposure to this language in the past, however most of my knowledge comes from the past two weeks of study!

CSS

CSS, Cascading Style Sheets, is the programming language of art. It allows programmers and myself to stylize the HTML in a website. This language can be used to turn any ordinary-looking website into a masterpiece! This language I've only had a grand total of two weeks of study in, however I am catching on very quickly and looking to broaden my horizons in in!

(Soon) Javascript

If HTML and CSS are what build a house, Javascript is what gives it electricity. Javascript is another key element in building a functioning, user-friendly website! As of now, I have very little experience in Javascript, but I am eager to get my foot in the door and learn what is possible with it!

Graphic Design

If there is one thing I am considered exceedingly good at by former peers, it's taking a photo and modifying it to a certain criteria! I have a year of advanced photoshop knowledge, which allows me to create and modify photos in almost any way! I look forward to using these abilities to greatly improve the graphical aspect of webpages I create!
